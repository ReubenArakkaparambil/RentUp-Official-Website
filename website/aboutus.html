About Us

How it started

Our student rental housing system aims to mitigate difficulties students face in finding suitable accommodations overseas. Founders developed a comprehensive platform to improve living experiences, provide accurate information, provide nearby amenities and speed up the process, promoting community and academic support.

Our Values

Welcoming
Our student rental housing system aims to mitigate difficulties students face in finding suitable accommodations overseas. Founders developed a comprehensive platform to improve living experiences, provide accurate information, provide nearby amenities and speed up the process, promoting community and academic support.

Personalized
RentUp offers personalized housing solutions for international students, local scholars, and advanced studies, focusing on individuality and tailoring to meet specific needs.

Connected
RentUp is a housing platform that connects students, fostering connections and fostering a sense of belonging, making roommate searches easier and fostering a supportive community.

Our Community

In every interaction, in every feature, and in every connection we help create, we welcome you to a housing experience that's not just streamlined, inclusive, and community-centric, but welcoming, personalized, and deeply connected. Your journey starts here, and we're here to make it uniquely yours. It goes beyond housing, offering electricity plans, SIM cards, broadband, and banking services, making it an all-in-one solution for a hassle-free student life journey.

Our Mission

To create a comprehensive platform that provides students with accurate property information, reduces stress, enhances housing search, fosters community, supports academic growth, and locates nearby amenities.
